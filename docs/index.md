# Pizza Tower

Pizza Tower game is a fast-paced platformer featuring unique art, creative levels, and a focus on speedrunning mechanics.

## What is Pizza Tower?

Pizza Tower game is an indie platformer inspired by classic titles, combining vibrant visuals and dynamic gameplay. Players navigate through various levels filled with obstacles, enemies, and secrets, all while aiming for the fastest completion times. The game’s design encourages exploration and experimentation, making each playthrough unique.

The art style of Pizza Tower is heavily influenced by 90s cartoons, providing a nostalgic yet fresh aesthetic. With a variety of characters and power-ups, the game enhances the traditional platforming experience, allowing players to develop their skills and strategies as they race against the clock.

## Key Features

- **Unique Art Style** — Vibrant visuals inspired by 90s cartoons.
- **Creative Levels** — Diverse environments with intricate designs and challenges.
- **Speedrunning Mechanics** — Focus on completing levels as quickly as possible.
- **Challenging Enemies** — Varied foes that require different strategies to defeat.
- **Power-Ups** — Special abilities that enhance gameplay and player performance.
- **Secret Areas** — Hidden zones that reward exploration and skill.
- **Dynamic Soundtrack** — Engaging music that complements the fast-paced action.
- **Replayability** — Multiple paths and strategies encourage repeated playthroughs.
